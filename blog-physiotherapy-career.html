Physiotherapy Career in India

Scope, Salary & Opportunities 2026

Growing Demand for Physiotherapists

The physiotherapy profession is experiencing unprecedented growth in India. With increasing awareness about physical rehabilitation, sports injuries, and lifestyle diseases, the demand for qualified physiotherapists has skyrocketed. India needs over 1 lakh physiotherapists by 2026.

Career Opportunities After BPT

Physiotherapy Specializations

Orthopedic PT

Treat musculoskeletal conditions, fractures, joint replacements

Neurological PT

Treat stroke, paralysis, Parkinson's, spinal cord injuries

Cardiopulmonary PT

Treat heart and lung conditions, post-surgical rehab

Pediatric PT

Treat children with developmental disorders

Sports PT

Treat sports injuries, work with athletes

Geriatric PT

Treat age-related conditions in elderly
