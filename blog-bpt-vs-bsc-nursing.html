BPT vs BSc Nursing

Which Course is Better? Complete Comparison 2026

One of the most common questions students ask is: "Should I choose BPT or BSc Nursing?" Both are excellent healthcare courses with great career prospects. This comparison will help you decide which is better for you.

Quick Comparison

Factor BPT BSc Nursing
Duration 4.5 Years 4 Years
Eligibility 10+2 PCB (50%) 10+2 PCB (45%)
Starting Salary Rs.4-6 LPA Rs.3.5-5 LPA
Job Type Mostly Private Govt + Private
Private Practice Easy to Start Difficult

Choose BPT If:

  • You're interested in physical therapy and rehabilitation
  • You want to start your own private practice
  • You're interested in sports medicine
  • You want to work independently
  • You prefer higher starting salary

Choose BSc Nursing If:

  • You're passionate about direct patient care
  • You want more job security
  • You're interested in government jobs
  • You want overseas opportunities
  • You prefer shorter course duration

Career Scope Comparison

BPT Career Options

Hospital PT, Sports PT, Private Clinic, Rehabilitation Centers

Rs.4-40 LPA

BSc Nursing Career

Staff Nurse, Govt Hospitals, Overseas, Nursing Educator

Rs.3.5-100 LPA

Our Verdict

Both courses have excellent career prospects. Choose based on your interest:

  • BPT is better if you want higher starting salary, private practice, and independence
  • BSc Nursing is better if you want job security, government jobs, and overseas opportunities
