Our hostel provides a home away from home for students. With separate hostels for boys and girls, we ensure a safe and comfortable living environment.

  • Spacious, well-ventilated rooms
  • 24/7 security with CCTV surveillance
  • Nutritious and hygienic food
  • Wi-Fi connectivity
  • Recreation room with TV and indoor games
  • Gym facility
  • Laundry service
  • Medical room with first aid
