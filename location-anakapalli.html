How to Reach from Anakapalli

Easy connectivity with multiple transport options

By Bus

Regular APSRTC buses from Anakapalli to Visakhapatnam. Get down at Marripalem stop. College is walking distance.

By Train

Anakapalli to Visakhapatnam local trains available. Auto from railway station to college (15 mins).

By Car/Bike

Via NH16 - approximately 45 km. Well-connected road with clear signage.
