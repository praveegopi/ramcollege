Best Nursing College Near NAD Junction

NAD Junction is a major transit hub in Visakhapatnam, connecting various parts of the city. Holy Faith College is just 20 minutes away, making it easily accessible from this central location.

We offer B.Sc Nursing, GNM, BPT, and B.Sc MLT courses with excellent infrastructure and placement support. Multiple bus routes connect NAD Junction to our college.

How to Reach from NAD Junction

  • By Bus: Routes 28, 38, 48, 55 towards Marripalem
  • By Car/Auto: 20 minutes via NAD Road → Marripalem
  • Nearest Landmark: Near Raman School, Jyothinagar
  • Travel Time: 15-25 minutes
