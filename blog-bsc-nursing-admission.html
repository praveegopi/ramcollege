BSc Nursing Admission 2026

Complete Guide to BSc Nursing Admission in Andhra Pradesh

BSc Nursing Eligibility Criteria

  • Passed 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and English
  • Minimum 45% aggregate marks in PCB (40% for SC/ST)
  • Age: Minimum 17 years, Maximum 35 years
  • Must be medically fit

Documents Required for Admission

Academic Documents

  • 10th Marksheet
  • 12th Marksheet
  • Transfer Certificate
  • Migration Certificate

Identity Documents

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Caste Certificate
  • Income Certificate
  • Domicile Certificate

Other Documents

  • Passport Photos (8)
  • Medical Certificate
  • Character Certificate
  • Bank Passbook Copy
