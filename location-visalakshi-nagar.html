Best Nursing College Near Visalakshi Nagar

Visalakshi Nagar is a peaceful residential area in Visakhapatnam, perfect for students seeking a quiet environment for their studies. Holy Faith College is just 18 minutes away, offering the best nursing and physiotherapy education.

Our college provides B.Sc Nursing, GNM, BPT, and B.Sc MLT courses with excellent placement opportunities. The residential location ensures safe and comfortable commute for students from Visalakshi Nagar.

How to Reach from Visalakshi Nagar

  • By Car/Auto: 18 minutes via Visalakshi Nagar Road → NH-16 → Marripalem
